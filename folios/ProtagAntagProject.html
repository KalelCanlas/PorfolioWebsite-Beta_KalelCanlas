Project Overview

Meet Timbre the Fallen God, and Siliman the Polar Terminator!
The God of creativity that had fallen from his purpose
And the mute child that will bring out the voice of the rest

The original characters I had built before my Gugma and Magabaya. These characters are not as serious as Gugma's in terms of story, but their concept and overall origin is heartfelt and very important I included the lore in the gallery.

Work Process

The design process exponential

Coming up with their overall design was interesting. I really want to be unconventional choosing a deaf character as the main hero as I really love hand-signs. Learning FSL is also a huge thing for me too! Same with music and creativity. Being mindful and creative can have a huge toll on you.

I wanted them to be expressive and tribal looking. I am still learning on that scope at the time. However, I did love the design for Siliman being Androgynous which I was aiming for. While Timbre's edgy look was not farfetched from what I expected.

Overall, they are a treat to work with. I will definitely go back and rework them for Inclusion when I am making my world-building more wider.

Reflection

Looking back, this project is a part of the growth I had with my Third Term Digital Illustration Endeavors. And I am proud to say that I am going where I want to go. And along that, I will enjoy the journey and the process, so that no matter the results, I can hold unto myself and the ground without suffocating myself.

I am finally growing more than I thought.
